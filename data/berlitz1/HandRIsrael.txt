        Jerusalem

        East Jerusalem; Tel. (02) 627 9777, fax (02) 627 9779. Arguably the

        breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Open-air bar, poolside restaurant, dining

        superior rooms are well worth the extra charge. 84 rooms.
