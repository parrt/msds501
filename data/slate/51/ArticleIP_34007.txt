               A little
about Mike:
            

            
Michael Kinsley formed 
                  Slate
                in June 1996. Previously, he was
senior editor at The New Republic in Washington, D.C., and co-host of the
CNN's Crossfire , for which he continues to act as a substitute
co-host. He is a contributing writer at Time magazine and
has also been editor of Harper's magazine, managing editor of the Washington
Monthly , and American survey editor of The Economist in
London.
